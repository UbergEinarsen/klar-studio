# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Owners of small local Norwegian businesses (cafés, tradespeople, clinics, clubs) who are
weak or invisible online. Often non-technical, time-poor, deciding fast whether this studio
is credible and worth a no-obligation chat. They reach the site from a referral, a local
search, or social, usually on mobile.

## Product Purpose

Marketing landing page for Klar Studio — a two-person studio that builds fast websites and
gets local businesses visible on Google Maps. The page must convert a curious visitor into a
contact-form submission or email. Longer term the studio intends to grow into a full
marketing agency, so the brand should read as ambitious and capable, not hobbyist.

## Brand Personality

Fast, confident, local-craftsman. Voice is direct, plain Norwegian, a little playful
("Klar, ferdig — på nett" riffs on "klar, ferdig, gå"). It should feel like a sharp modern
Scandinavian studio with energy and momentum — trustworthy first, distinctive second.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS template (electric-blue-on-white that belongs to any startup). Cheap local-web
agency clip-art. Editorial-magazine serif/italic affectation. Cream/beige "warm minimal"
slop. Stock-metric hero template (big number + small label).

## Design Principles

- **Practice what you preach** — a studio that sells fast, sharp websites must ship the
  sharpest, fastest site in its market. The page is the portfolio.
- **Momentum is the brand** — "klar, ferdig, på nett" is a three-beat rhythm; let speed and
  motion be felt, not just claimed.
- **Trust before flair** — local owners buy from people they believe in. Drama serves
  credibility; it never undercuts it.
- **Honest proof** — show real work and real claims, not invented stats.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Norwegian (`lang="nb"`). WCAG AA contrast on all text. Full reduced-motion alternatives
(video, reveals, kinetic elements all degrade). Keyboard-navigable, visible focus rings,
no-JS content fallback.
